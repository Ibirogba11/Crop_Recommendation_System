Why Soil Components Matter

Understanding the chemical makeup of your land is the difference between a failing field and a bumper harvest.

Nitrogen (N)

Responsible for leaf growth and green color. Essential for photosynthesis.

Ideal for Grains: 40-60% of total N-P-K

Phosphorus (P)

Focuses on root development and flower/seed production.

Ideal for Tubers: Higher P required

Potassium (K)

Regulates water movement and boosts disease resistance.

Critical for fruit quality and size

pH Level

Controls how easily plants can "drink" the nutrients available.

Neutral Range: 6.0 - 7.5
